Ismail Geles

I am a Master's student at the Graz University of Technology in Information and Computer Engineering, focusing on AI, Computer Vision and Robotics.

I am currently writing my Master's thesis at the Autonomous Systems Lab (ASL) at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology ETH Zurich on the topic of aerial manipulation through Reinforcement Learning.

Before that, I was a graduate exchange student at the University of Waterloo in Canada. In 2021, I received my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from Graz University of Technology.
